Short Film

In July 2019 I made a short film with the team in IAT202 New Media Images at Simon Fraser University.

Logline

A deeply-in-debt gambler tries all the ways to make his wife cry after he realizes that she could cry diamonds. However, he finds a shocking truth about his wife that changes him completely.

THE PREPATATION PROCESS

Previous Next

To complete this film, my team and I re-written the script five times. We design the first logline as "A deeply-in-debt gambler husband tries all the ways to make his wife cry after he realizes that his wife could tear diamonds out of her eyes. A Tragedy, however, is brewing as he goes further on treating his wife worse and worse." We talked about this vision with our professor. He thought this vision is to down so we changed.

Here is our final script.

I define this film as conflict, and introspection——these are the two characteristics of this film. I used the orange gradient palette. Dark yellow tones can cause tension and irritability to create dramatic conflict.

IMPLEMENTATION PROCESS

I use the Facebook group to do the casting call. We received more than 30 resumes of actors on the same day. In the end, we selected two Asian actors who fit our film.

I used BMPCC4K and FUJIFILM XT-2 to shot this film.

We have a clear film log and plan, so we finished shooting in two days. After discussing with the professor, we changed the ending. In the previous version, our ending was too long, so we decided to edit the ending into an open ending. Therefore we used another day to reshot the end of the film.

THE CHANLLAGE

Dealing with everyone's time

We need to complete a short movie in one month. Four members with different schedules need to have a production schedule to set the deadline. Therefore we have this schedule.

THE AWARD

Our films received these three awards in comparison to the 13 films in this course.

Through communication and cooperation in this project, I was able to manage related details and find the balance between this project and others.

If we still have a second chance, we will spend more time on sound production. We did not use professional recording equipment when producing the sound, and eventually, the sound of foley was too small, this part made us lose some scores.
